4 Motor Control Development Kits and Boards

Introduction

Microchip provides a range of development kits and reference designs to help you simplify and accelerate your motor control application projects.

With QSpin, you can easily select these boards and seamlessly configure the FOC algorithm. Additionally, you can configure your own custom board in just a few simple steps, providing flexibility and customization to meet your specific needs.

Development Kits

Our development kits are designed to offer cost-effective method of evaluating and developing 3-phase sensored or sensorless FOC applications projects . These kits include everything you need to get started with your motor control projects.

  • dsPICDEM MCLV-2 Development Board
  • dsPICDEM MCHV-3 Development Board
  • MCLV-48V-300W Development Board

Motor Control Dual In-line Modules (DIM)

The Motor Control Dual In-line Modules (DIM) provide a compact and efficient solution for motor control applications. These modules can be easily swapped and configured for MCLV-48V-300W development board.

  • SAM C21 Motor Control DIM
  • PIC32CM MC Motor Control DIM
  • SAM E54 Motor Control DIM
  • SAM E70 Motor Control DIM
  • PIC32MK MCM Motor Control DIM
  • PIC32MK MCA Motor Control DIM

Motor Control Plug In-line Modules (PIM)

The Motor Control Plug In-line Modules (PIM) offer a flexible and modular approach to motor control. These modules can be easily swapped and configured for dsPICDEM MCLV2 and dsPICDEM MCHV3 development board.

  • ATSAMC21 Motor Control Plug-in Module
  • PIC32CM MC Motor Control Plug-in Module
  • ATSAME54 Motor Control Plug-in Module
  • ATSAME70 Motor Control Plug-in Module
  • PIC32MK MCM Motor Control Plug-in Module

Configure Custom Board

QSpin allows you to configure the peripherals for your custom board by following few simple steps. Refer Configure your custom motor control board for details.
